This website is now archived. The  company operated between 2002 and March 2022 when the co-founders retired.

Charles Beagrie Limited was an independent management consultancy company specialising in the digital archive, library, science and research sectors. Our areas of expertise were:

  • Science and Innovation
  • Digital Libraries and Data Archives
  • Research Data Management, Digital Preservation, and Digital Curation
  • Strategy and Policy, Business Planning, Stakeholder Engagement, and Evaluation and Impact Assessment

Our services included:

  • Business Cases and Cost-Benefit Analyses
  • Evaluation and Economic Impact Studies
  • National and Corporate Strategies
  • Research and Feasibility Studies, and Research Project Partnerships
  • Stakeholder Surveys, Focus Groups, and Interviews
  • Requirements and Market Appraisal for Digital Preservation Systems

We were based in the UK and worked globally. You can take a look at what we offered and what we  achieved for our clients on this website.

The company devoted part of its resources each year to promoting educational aims. This website provides archived resources in our areas of expertise that can be used by students and staff in informatics, library, or archive studies.
